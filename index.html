Common Internet Issues

Slow Internet Speed

Slow internet speed can be caused by network congestion, poor signal strength, outdated hardware, or excessive background data usage.

Connectivity Issues

Connectivity issues occur when your device cannot maintain a stable internet connection. This could result from faulty cables, router problems, or service outages.

Cyber Threats

Cyber threats like phishing, malware, and ransomware are risks that compromise your data security. Always use strong passwords and updated security software.
